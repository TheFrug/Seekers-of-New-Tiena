Brenwin's Caravan is a merchants caravan that has traveled far from its origin in [[Al-Qonidar]] and is currently stopped in [[Bromzheath]]. The caravan is led by [[Brenwin Al-avik]], a half-dragonborn prince.

Brenwin's Caravan is made up of six wagons led by two [[Al-Qonidarian Zebra]] each.
