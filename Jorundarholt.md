Jorundarholt is the current name for a recent settlement in [[Skiptaheimr]]. This settlement was constructed not even two years ago, and currently subsists on fishing and hunting in the nearby woods, which appear to stretch on for miles and miles. In accordance to the tradition appeared upon by the many clans of Skiptaheimr, there are representatives from many clans living in Jorundarholt and observing its progress. 

Every two weeks, someone from the town is allowed to suggest a new name, and the townsfolk will try it out. 

Previous names: 
Stalfold, Blarvegr, Amaelissamrvegr, Meinlaussheimr, Jokullland, Kynligreverold
