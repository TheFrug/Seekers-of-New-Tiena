# Suryis

## Description
- **Name:** Suryis
- **Race/Species:** [[Minotaur]]
- **Gender:** Male
- **Age:** 50
- **Occupation/Class:** Gladiator, Cultist 
- **Affiliation:** [[Cult of Eternal Flame]], [[Unseelie Court]]
- **Alignment:** Chaotic Evil
- **Appearance:** Glowing red horns that curl and point out, long braided hair between them and running down the sides of his face, always shirtless, and carrying a greataxe on his back, wearing a red medallion.

## Summary
Suryis is an Emberhorn minotaur from the [[Unseelie Court|Unseelie]] [[Feywild]]. Hailing from the Burnwillows, he worships [[Imix]], the evil elemental incarnation of fire. Suryis, like many monstrous fae, was designed by the Unseelie for combat, and as such has lived much of his life taking part in gladiatorial combat and war. However, Suryis has a softer side, which he has yet to nurture as a result of being forced to participate in violence his whole life, and he pines for love.

## Roleplaying
 - **Wants and Needs:** To impress others and be the strongest
 - **Secret or Obstacle:** Is secretly a passionate romantic who pines for love
 - **Significant Flaw:** Worships Imix, the Eternal Flame, mainly because this is the only fire deity he knows of. Imix is super evil, and the power he grants is contingent on his followers occasionally committing acts of arson.
 - **Carrying:** Charred leather satchel containing a pressed wildflower from the Burnwillows, a small stone shaped like a heart that is resistant to any kinds of heat
