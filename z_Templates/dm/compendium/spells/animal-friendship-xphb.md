# Animal Friendship
%%-- Embedded content starts on the next line. --%%
*1st-level, Enchantment*  

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** 30 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (a morsel of food)
- **Duration:** 24 hours

Target a Beast that you can see within range. The target must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or have the [Charmed](rules/conditions.md#Charmed) condition for the duration. If you or one of your allies deals damage to the target, the spells ends.

**Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.** You can target one additional Beast for each spell slot level above 1.

**Classes**: [Bard](compendium/lists/list-spells-classes-bard.md); [Druid](compendium/lists/list-spells-classes-druid.md); [Bard (College of Lore)](compendium/lists/list-spells-classes-bard-xphb-college-of-lore-xphb.md "subclass=XPHB;class=XPHB"); [Ranger](compendium/lists/list-spells-classes-ranger.md)
