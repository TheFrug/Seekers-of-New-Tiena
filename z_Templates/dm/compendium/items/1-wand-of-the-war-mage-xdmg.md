# +1 Wand of the War Mage
*Uncommon (requires attunement by a spellcaster)*  

- **Weight**: 1.0 lbs.

While holding this wand, you gain a +1 bonus to spell attack rolls. In addition, you ignore [Half Cover](rules/variant-rules/cover-xphb.md) when making a spell attack roll.

*Source: SRD 5.2*
