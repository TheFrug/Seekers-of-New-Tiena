# Circlet of Blasting
*Wondrous item, uncommon*  


While wearing this circlet, you can cast [Scorching Ray](compendium/spells/scorching-ray-xphb.md) with it (`+5` to hit). The circlet can't cast this spell again until the next dawn.

*Source: SRD 5.2*
