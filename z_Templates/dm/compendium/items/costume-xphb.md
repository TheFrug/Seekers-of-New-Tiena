# Costume
*Adventuring gear*  

- **Cost**: 5 gp
- **Weight**: 4.0 lbs.

While wearing a Costume, you have [Advantage](rules/variant-rules/advantage-xphb.md) on any ability check you make to impersonate the person or type of person it represents.
