# +3 Wand of the War Mage
*Very rare (requires attunement by a spellcaster)*  

- **Weight**: 1.0 lbs.

While holding this wand, you gain a +3 bonus to spell attack rolls. In addition, you ignore [Half Cover](rules/variant-rules/cover-xphb.md) when making a spell attack roll.
