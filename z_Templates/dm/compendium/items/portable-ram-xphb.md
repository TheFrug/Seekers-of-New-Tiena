# Portable Ram
*Adventuring gear*  

- **Cost**: 4 gp
- **Weight**: 35.0 lbs.

You can use a Portable Ram to break down doors. When doing so, you gain a +4 bonus to the Strength check. One other character can help you use the ram, giving you [Advantage](rules/variant-rules/advantage-xphb.md) on this check.

*Source: SRD 5.2*
