# Crystal Ball
*Wondrous item, very rare (requires attunement)*  

- **Weight**: 3.0 lbs.

The typical crystal ball, a very rare item, is about 6 inches in diameter. While touching it, you can cast the [Scrying](compendium/spells/scrying-xphb.md) spell (save DC 17) with it.

*Source: SRD 5.2*
