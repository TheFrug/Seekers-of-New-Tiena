# Ring of Three Wishes
*Legendary*  


While wearing this ring, you can expend 1 of its 3 charges to cast [Wish](compendium/spells/wish-xphb.md) from it. The ring becomes nonmagical when you use the last charge.

*Source: SRD 5.2*
