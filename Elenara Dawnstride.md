## Summary
Elenara is an elf from [[Alfheim]] using a disguise to hide in the town of [[Jorundarholt]]. She was given this task by the elders of Alfheim, who wanted to ascertain the threat that [[Jorundarholt]] and its people posed, but Elenara has an ulterior motive for volunteering, which is to get revenge against [[Kolbein Ironfist]] on behalf of her sister, [[Lirael Dawnstride]].

Elenara takes on the false name of [[Ynglinga]], and advertises herself as one of the young buxom barmaids that love to serve drinks in the [[Tipsy Warbler]].
