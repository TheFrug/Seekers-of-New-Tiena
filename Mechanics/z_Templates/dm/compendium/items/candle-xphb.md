# Candle
*Adventuring gear*  

- **Cost**: 1 cp

For 1 hour, a lit Candle sheds [Bright Light](bright-light-xphb.md) in a 5-foot radius and [Dim Light](dim-light-xphb.md) for an additional 5 feet.

*Source: SRD 5.2*
