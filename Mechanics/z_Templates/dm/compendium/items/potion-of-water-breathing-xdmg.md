# Potion of Water Breathing
*Potion, uncommon*  

- **Weight**: 0.5 lbs.

You can breathe underwater for 24 hours after drinking this potion.

This potion's cloudy green fluid smells of the sea and has a jellyfish-like bubble floating in it.
