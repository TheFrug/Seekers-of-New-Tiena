# Military Saddle
*Tack and harness*  

- **Cost**: 20 gp
- **Weight**: 30.0 lbs.

A saddle comes with a bit, a bridle, reins, and any other equipment needed to use the saddle. A Military Saddle gives [Advantage](advantage-xphb.md) on any ability check you make to remain mounted.
